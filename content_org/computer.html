the multiline comments

There are two ways to generate multiline comments first is using the escape sequence. \\n 

fat_cat ='''
I'll do a list:
\t* Cat food
\t* Fishies
\t* Catnip\n\t* Grass
'''

thin_mouse ='''
I'll do another list:
\t* mouse food
\t* Rice
\t* Catnip\n\t* Grass
'''
print(fat_cat)
print(thin_mouse)
